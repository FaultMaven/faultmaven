## Summary

This PR introduces a comprehensive README rewrite (`README_BETA_DRAFT.md`) that focuses entirely on **community collaboration and genuine feedback-seeking** during the public beta phase, with zero commercial messaging.

**Core Philosophy:** "Help us build FaultMaven" — focusing solely on feedback and utility as the most authentic and effective strategy for community validation.

## Messaging Philosophy

This README embodies: **"Focusing solely on feedback and utility is the most authentic and effective strategy for a community validation phase."**

The best way to get honest feedback is to remove the commercial question entirely. This approach:
- Makes beta participation about **product improvement**, not commercial benefits
- Rewards contributors with **recognition and influence**, not discounts
- Asks for input on **sustainability and governance**, not pricing
- Validates **real problem-solving**, not business model
